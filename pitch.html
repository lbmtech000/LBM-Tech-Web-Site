01 / 07 The Problem Critical Infrastructure Gap
🔇

Running Blind

Most desalination plants in Algeria have no real-time monitoring. Operators rely on manual inspection — slow, inaccurate, dangerous.

⚠️

Reactive Failures

Without predictive tools, equipment fails unexpectedly. A single shutdown can cut water supply to thousands of households for days.

⚡

Energy Waste

Desalination is energy-intensive. Without optimization, plants consume 15–30% more energy than necessary.

Algeria alone has 11+ major seawater desalination plants supplying millions of people — and most have no digital monitoring layer.
