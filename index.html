LBM is a technology startup — not a desalination company. We don't build new plants; we modernize existing ones. We install IoT sensor systems, connect them to our monitoring platform, and progressively add AI features like predictive maintenance and energy optimization.

North Africa has one of the highest concentrations of seawater desalination capacity in Africa, and much of that infrastructure is aging and unmonitored.
